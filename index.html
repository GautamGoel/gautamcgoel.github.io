Gautam Goel

I am a PhD student in the Computing and Mathematical Sciences (CMS) department at Caltech, where I am extremely fortunate to be advised by Babak Hassibi. I am broadly interested in machine learning, optimization, and the analysis of algorithms; especially 1) online learning, 2) non-convex optimization, and 3) randomized algorithms.
